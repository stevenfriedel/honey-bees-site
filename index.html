Welcome

My Services

What We Offer

We operate Monday-Friday providing childcare to ages 0-12, offering anything from before and after school care including school runs and homework clubs to full day complete childcare. 30 hour free childcare funding for 2, 3 and 4 year olds also available.

Our Allotment

We have a wonderful allotment plot with a new edition in the form of a summerhouse, enabling us to spend time here in all weather conditions. We learn how to grow (and eat!) our own fruit and vegetables and it gets the children involved with the community, taking part in things like the yearly flower show.

Activities

We enjoy a wide range of activities that encourage children to develop and learn. Activities are planned around cirriculum, covering all areas of learning whilst allowing the children to enjoy and explore freely. Ranging from learning about nature and exploring local woods, canals and countryside to counting, storytime and sensory play and everything in between.

Where We Are

I am located in Eccleshill, Bradford, West Yorkshire. I regularly serve the BD2/3/10 postcodes, but if you are in any other postcodes feel free to enquire as I may still be able to offer some services.
